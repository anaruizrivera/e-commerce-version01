Accessories

Our curated space is complete with lifestyle and fashion accessories to compliment your individual look. Our aim is to provide that perfect accessory, be it minimal or directional. We carefully and lovingly select our accessories from near and far, with an emphasis on NZ made and artisan quality. Our range includes fragrant soy candles from loved brand George & Edi, Flash Jewellery, SOPHIE, Saben, QUAY Sunglasses and MOOI Bags. Silk&Steel, along with local designer Odi make for edgy, designer jewellery, which compliment our selection of Rosefield Watches- from NYC & Amsterdam. Fun, affordable add-ons include Lindi Kingi Design, Commoners unisex leather essentials and Koko Body- vegan, cruelty free body products.


Dresses


Hebe showcases the chic, casual and refined. Dresses of organic cotton and silk for day can be found in our curated space from considered, NZ lead labels such as Kowtow, NYNE, Blak, Marle and Twenty Seven Names. Bold, beautiful pieces for the individual dresser are readily available with monthly deliveries from labels such as COOP, Ketz-ke and in-store brand My Boyfriends Back. To balance our mix of luxe and casual, affordable basics are always covered from Betty Basics, Foxwood, Commoners and Assembly Label- their crews and slogan tees are our fave!

Tops

Unique separates, luxe tops and quality tees are among the favourites at Hebe. Build your wardrobe from essential layering pieces with organic cotton and quality handle in mind. Core styles are covered from fresh tees to singlets, sheer over-pieces, cute blouses and tailored, crisp shirts. We love to appreciate the classic fundamentals of a good basic while also indulging in the 'on point'.
